Currently, I am a Research Scientist in the Audio research team at Pindrop. My role involves the development of a robust deepfake detection system, aimed at addressing real-world challenges. My research interests include Deepfake detection, Speaker recognition and Anti-spoofing.

I graduated (Ph.D.) from Sorbonne University, France, worked in the Audio Security and Privacy group, supervised by Prof. Nicholas Evans. I have interned in the Voice-ID group at Amazon, USA in 2022.
